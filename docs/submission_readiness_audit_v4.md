## Evidence Added In v4

- Implemented deterministic local human-correction benchmark.
- Main evaluation: 53,760 rollouts across five splits, eight methods, four tasks, and seven seeds.
- Ablation evaluation: 9,408 rollouts on the combined hard-shift split.
- Stress evaluation: 117,600 rollouts over human noise, correction delay, overcorrection bias, intent ambiguity, and combined stress.
- Figures and CSVs are regenerated by `python src\run_experiment.py`.

## Main Hard-Split Evidence

- `minimum_intervention_learner`: task success `0.56919 +/- 0.02468`; correction efficiency `0.76851`; damage `0.02976`.
- `uncertainty_query_policy`: task success `0.53720 +/- 0.02611`; correction efficiency `0.60334`; damage `0.07812`.
- Paired task-success difference versus uncertainty querying: `0.03199 +/- 0.04562`.
- Paired correction-efficiency difference versus preference-only ranker: `0.12153 +/- 0.04126`.
- `oracle_minimal_correction`: task success `0.65774 +/- 0.02258`.

## Ablation Issue

The efficiency mechanism is supported, but task success does not uniquely validate the full objective.

- Full learner: task success `0.56919 +/- 0.02468`; efficiency `0.76851`; damage `0.02976`.
- Minus human-effort cost: task success `0.56994 +/- 0.03428`; efficiency `0.71189`; damage `0.06176`.

The full objective improves efficiency and damage, but not task success.

## Submission Readiness Blockers

- Non-decisive paired task-success margin against uncertainty querying.
- Full objective is validated by tradeoff metrics rather than success dominance.
- Local synthetic evidence only; no real robot, high-fidelity simulator, or accepted external benchmark.

## Revival Requirements

- Evaluate on real human-in-the-loop robot correction data or accepted high-fidelity interactive manipulation benchmarks.
- Learn the boundary/correction model from real correction traces.
- Compare against modern active learning, DAgger, preference learning, residual correction, and safety-filter baselines.
- Demonstrate decisive paired gains in task success while preserving lower human burden and damage.
